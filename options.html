LasTabZ Preferences
If you are enjoying LasTabZ functionality rate it 5 stars here

Menu Tips: Midle click and drag tabs in the menu to close multiple tabs. Drag outside of the menu and release middle mouse button to cancel. Pressing the X on any selected tabs will close all selected tabs! To deselect, close a different tab. If you mouse up on any tab the selected tabs will close.

Remember, if you close something accidentally, you can press ctrl-shift-t to reopen closed tabs or right click next to the tab bar to find this feature in the contextual menu.

Tab Tips: If you do not want to jump to your previous tab when closing your current tab, either dissable the feature above, or select the tab you want before middle clicking the one you want close. You will not be transported to your last tab when closing tabs with this feature enabled unless you close your currently selected tab.
